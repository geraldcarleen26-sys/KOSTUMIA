About Kostumia

Wear Once. Loved Again. / Vaa Mara Moja. Pendwa Tena.

Our Story

Kostumia was born from a simple idea: beautiful occasion wear deserves more than one moment in the spotlight. Every wedding dress, graduation gown, and traditional outfit tells a story — and those stories shouldn't end after a single event.

We connect sellers who want to give their outfits a second life with buyers looking for quality, affordable occasion wear. It's sustainable fashion that celebrates culture, style, and community.

Our Mission

To make sustainable fashion accessible, elegant, and joyful — in both English and Kiswahili, for every community we serve.
